About Me

I hold a B.Tech in Electronics & Communication and a Master’s in Digital Communication, which laid the foundation for my professional journey. Over the years, I gained valuable experience in teaching, project coordination, and HR—each role sharpening my ability to analyze, communicate, and adapt to challenges.

Beyond the technical side, I’m passionate about writing, teaching, storytelling, and creativity, often channeling my thoughts and experiences into words that inspire and connect. After taking a career break for family, I am now returning with renewed focus and determination.

I chose data science not because it is an emerging field, but because I discovered it to be the perfect intersection of my skills, experiences, and passion. Through self-reflection, I realized that working with data, solving problems, and creating impact is what excites me the most. I truly love data science—because doing what you love is not only fulfilling, it’s also where you create your best work. For me, data science is not just a career choice; it is a path that feels naturally aligned with who I am and what I genuinely enjoy.
