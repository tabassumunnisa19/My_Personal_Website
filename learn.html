HR Interview Questions

Q1. Tell me about yourself

Answer: I am a [Your Qualification] with experience in [Your Field]. I enjoy solving problems and applying my skills to real-world scenarios. I am particularly excited about this role at TCS because it aligns with my career goals.

Tip: Keep a 30–60 second summary. Focus on achievements relevant to the job.

Q2. Why do you want to join our company?

Answer: I admire TCS’s culture of innovation and learning. The company offers opportunities to work on diverse projects, and my skills align with the company’s goals.

Tip: Mention specific programs, projects, or values after research.

Q3. Where do you see yourself in 5 years?

Answer: I see myself taking on increasing responsibilities, contributing to challenging projects, and growing into a role combining technical expertise with leadership skills.

Tip: Show ambition but stay realistic.

Q1. Tell me about your biggest weakness

Answer: One area I am improving is [Weakness]. I have been taking steps like [Actions] to overcome it and turn it into a strength.

Tip: Be honest but show initiative for self-improvement.

Q2. Give an example of a mistake you made and what you learned

Answer: In a previous project, I [Describe Mistake]. I learned [Lesson] and now apply that knowledge to avoid similar mistakes.

Tip: Highlight accountability and learning.

Q1. Why did you choose Data Science as a career path?

Answer: I enjoy analyzing data to extract insights and solve problems. Data Science combines my programming, statistical, and analytical skills to make a real business impact.

Tip: Connect your answer to projects or learning experiences.

Q2. Tell me about a data science project you worked on and your role

Answer: I worked on [Project Name], where the goal was [Project Goal]. My role involved data cleaning, feature engineering, and building predictive models. We achieved [Outcome], improving [Business/Process].

Tip: Use STAR method: Situation → Task → Action → Result.
