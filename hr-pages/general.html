General / IT HR Interview Questions & Answers

  1. Tell me about yourself.

    Good afternoon, my name is Tabassum Unnisa. I have completed my Master’s in Digital Communication Engineering and have professional experience in teaching, project coordination, and HR. These roles have helped me build strong analytical, problem-solving, and people management skills. Originally from Bangalore, I am currently based in Kochi. At present, I am focusing on Data Science and aiming to apply my technical knowledge and multidisciplinary background to deliver impactful insights and solutions.

  2. Why do you want to join our company?

    I admire your company’s strong reputation in the IT industry and its focus on innovation and employee growth. I believe the values and opportunities here align with my career goals in Data Science and IT, and I am confident I can contribute meaningfully while continuing to learn and grow.

  3. What do you know about our company?

    Your company is a recognized leader in IT services with global clients, known for innovation, large-scale digital transformation, and a people-first culture. I appreciate the way you focus on career growth and cutting-edge technology solutions.

  4. Where do you see yourself in 5 years?

    I see myself as a skilled professional taking ownership of projects, mentoring juniors, and contributing to impactful solutions in Data Science and IT. My goal is to grow both technically and in leadership roles.

  5. Why should we hire you?

    I bring a strong academic background, professional experience across multiple roles, and an ability to adapt quickly. My combination of technical expertise, problem-solving skills, and people management experience makes me well-suited to contribute to both technical and collaborative aspects of the role.

  6. If you get an offer from another big company (Google, Microsoft, etc.), will you still join us?

    I value stability and long-term growth. While big names are attractive, what matters most to me is the opportunity to learn, contribute, and grow in a supportive environment. If selected here, I will be committed to building my career with your company.

  7. Are you open to relocation?

    Yes, I am flexible with relocation, as I understand projects and opportunities can be across different locations. I see it as an opportunity to grow personally and professionally.

  8. Are you comfortable working in shifts?

    Yes, I am open to working in shifts if required, as I understand this is part of IT industry operations and global client requirements.

  9. How do you handle working under pressure?

    I stay calm, prioritize tasks, and break down work into manageable steps. I believe pressure can be managed by planning, time management, and clear communication with the team.

  10. Tell me about a time when you worked in a team and faced conflict.

    In a project, I once faced a conflict regarding task ownership. I suggested an open discussion where each member could share concerns. By clarifying responsibilities and focusing on common goals, we resolved the issue and completed the project successfully.

  11. What are your salary expectations?

    I am open to the standard salary range for this role based on industry standards and my skills. My main focus is on learning and contributing effectively.

  12. What are your strengths and weaknesses?

    My strengths include adaptability, problem-solving, and communication. A weakness I am working on is perfectionism—I sometimes spend extra time fine-tuning work, but I am learning to balance quality with efficiency.

  13. If selected, how soon can you join us?

    I am available to join immediately / within the standard notice period, depending on requirements.

  14. What do you know about our competitors?

    I understand that your competitors are other leading IT service providers in India and globally. However, your company stands out for its client relationships, employee culture, and innovation focus.

  15. What are your career goals in IT?

    My goal is to grow as a Data Science professional while also understanding the broader IT industry, contributing to impactful projects, and gradually moving toward leadership roles.
